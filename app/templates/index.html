What is Small Fish?

Small Fish is an initiative that seeks to professionalize the work environment of NFT game players, offering infrastructure for their activity. The fundamental idea of SF is to democratize playing conditions in the NFT gaming ecosystem. Large investors have access to sophisticated software, that automate the purchase and sale of coins and even the activities in the games. On the other hand, other players do all their operations without the aid of tools or using rudimentary ones, such as manually filled spreadsheets. Our proposal is to offer software infrastructure that allows every player to focus on his activity, while having control over his investments.

SF's business model is based on a subscription service that gives access to a set of web apps. To see available apps, click on the Apps link on the home page. If any app interests you, click on Subscribe and subscribe to our service. The subscription gives you access to all SF apps, including those yet to be released. A list of Apps in development can be found in the Roadmap section.

Our philosophy is based on customer focus and, for this reason, we maintain permanent communication and support channels. Click on the Contact link to access our social networks. There you can send suggestions, report problems or difficulties in using the apps and praise them.

Check Wallet

Check Wallet is a tool that allows the user to check the Metamask balance in any chosen feat currency. Balance checking is available in three modes: crypto, game tokens and full report. The crypto mode allows accessing the balance of the Metamask in the main cryptocurrencies, BNB, Bitcoin and Ethereum. To access the results, the user only needs to provide the public address of Metamask.

In the game tokens mode, in addition to the public address of the Metamask, it is necessary to inform which games the user would like to check the balance of tokens. It is important to note that the CW return in this case refers to the balance of tokens on Metamask and thus excludes the balance of tokens invested in the game. Soon, it will be possible to estimate the total investment of the user in the games with the Track Wallet, a tool to be launched by SF, according to Roadmap.

Finally, by selecting the full report option, the user will have access to the balance of crypto and game tokens on the selected Metamask.
